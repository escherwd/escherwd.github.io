Living with a broken camera can be very inconvenient and frustrating. Luckily, rear and front cameras are upon the quickest parts to swap out on an iPhone of any model. The new camera can be put in and the phone can be sealed up in less than 5 minutes.

PURCHASING A NEW CAMERA

As much as we wish we could put in an iPhone 7 camera into an iPhone 5c, we can't. Phone parts are extremely specialized, and cameras are no exception. Always make sure you buy the camera for your phone model specifically if you choose to purchase it yourself (otherwise I will purchase it).

When the camera is ordered, it arrives with a plastic piece protecting the lens (like pictured). This means that the camera will have never been smudged and will be in pristine quality when installed to the phone. To the left: the new camera, to the right: the old one taken out of an iPhone 6s.
